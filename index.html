Paint Correction & Ceramic Coating

True protection starts with the paint. Paint correction removes years of swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation to bring back a deep, mirror-like finish — then ceramic coating seals it in with a durable, hydrophobic layer that guards against UV rays, road grime, and water spotting. Together they deliver lasting protection, showroom-level gloss, and longevity that ordinary waxes simply can't match.

🛡️

Protection

A hard, hydrophobic barrier shields your paint from UV rays, road grime, contaminants, and minor scratches.

✨

Gloss

Multi-stage correction restores depth, clarity, and color richness for a true mirror finish.

⏳

Longevity

Years of durable protection that keeps your vehicle looking cleaner and newer for longer.

Ceramic coating provides long-lasting protection while enhancing your vehicle's gloss and depth. It forms a durable, hydrophobic layer over your paint, helping repel water, dirt, UV rays, and road grime — making your car easier to maintain and keeping it looking cleaner for longer.

Every application starts with proper preparation. We begin with a full exterior wash and paint decontamination to remove embedded contaminants. This is followed by a multi-stage paint correction to eliminate swirl marks and imperfections, refining the paint to a flawless finish. After a detailed panel prep, the ceramic coating is applied for maximum bonding and durability.

We offer full-vehicle protection, including paint, wheels, glass, and interior surfaces — helping reduce buildup, improve visibility, and protect against everyday wear.

Ceramic coating isn't just about shine — it's about preserving your vehicle and keeping it looking its best long-term.

Every service starts with a full exterior wash and paint decontamination to remove embedded contaminants. We then perform a multi-stage machine polish (between 1–3 steps, depending on the condition) to safely level defects and refine the paint to a flawless finish.
